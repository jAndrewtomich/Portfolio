Python List Comprehension

Like most naive new programmers, I embraced the 'For' loop eagerly.  I was thrilled that I could perform iterative operations and didn't think to question the implementation or its applications until I had personally run aground on its limitations a frustrating number of times.  In addition, it began to dawn on me that, at least thus far, the most frequent use-case for employing a loop structure was simply the generation or creation of a list.  Python offers a compact alternative: List Comprehension.
